About AquaTrack

AquaTrack monitors three critical water quality parameters using IoT sensors with WiFi transmission:

  • 🌡️ Temperature - Monitors water temperature for optimal conditions
  • 💎 TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) - Measures mineral content and purity
  • 🌫️ Turbidity - Detects water clarity and suspended particles
